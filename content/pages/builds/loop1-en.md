### Overview

The "Loop1" is a compact true-bypass loop switching pedal controllable via MIDI. It can be made by boxing an [Oscillator Devices Imp-3PDT](https://oscillatordevices.com/imp-midi/#Imp-3PDT) into a 1590A.
